# Strength

## Description

A measure of your raw physical power. It affects how much you can carry and the damage of all melee attacks. It also provides a small bonus to your overall hit points.

## Derived Statistics and Effects

- Carry Weight  
  *= 130 + (STR x 20), lbs*

- Hit Points  
  *= 10 + STR + (END x 2) (≥ 8)*

- Melee Damage  
  *= 4 + STR (≥ 1), 1 die*

## Associated Skills

- Melee Weapons
